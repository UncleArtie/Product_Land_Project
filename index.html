Ponyard Kipling's Random Quote Generator

Features

Image

The Ponyard Kipling Random Quote Generator allows a user to generate a random quote by the press of a simple button. The generator then pulls a quote by random choice from the collected stories of Ponyard Kipling known as "The Animal Annals of the Atomic Age" or alternately, "Stories Just Meh." The user is then presented with the quote chosen for their amusement, and may, if they decide, Tweet the random to quote to their Twitter account.


How It Works

Image

The Ponyard Kipling Random Quote Generator pulls random quotes from a database of 365 quotes, that's right, 365 quotes! That's enough quotes for an entire year, and each quote comes from a different story. No two quotes are from the same story meaning all quotes are original in origin.
